### 加强预期引导，提高消费信心
---
```mermaid

sequenceDiagram
    participant Government as 政府
    participant PolicyCommunication as 政策沟通
    participant PublicOpinion as 舆论引导
    participant Market as 市场
    participant Society as 社会

    rect rgba(173, 216, 230, 0.3) # 加强政策沟通和解读，浅蓝色背景
        Government ->> PolicyCommunication: 加强政策沟通和解读
        note over Government,PolicyCommunication: 提升政策透明度和稳定性
        activate PolicyCommunication
        PolicyCommunication -->> PolicyCommunication: 增强宏观政策透明度
        note left of PolicyCommunication: 及时回应社会关切
        PolicyCommunication -->> Market: 稳定市场预期
        note right of Market: 引导积极向好的发展预期
        deactivate PolicyCommunication
    end

    rect rgba(144, 238, 144, 0.3) # 营造良好舆论氛围，浅绿色背景
        Government ->> PublicOpinion: 营造良好舆论氛围
        note over Government,PublicOpinion: 提振社会信心
        activate PublicOpinion
        PublicOpinion -->> PublicOpinion: 加强正面宣传引导
        note left of PublicOpinion: 深入解读经济发展前景
        PublicOpinion -->> Society: 提振市场信心和社会信心
        note left of Society: 营造积极向上的社会氛围
        deactivate PublicOpinion
    end

    Society -->> Society: 凝聚社会共识
    note over Society: 共同推动经济高质量发展
```
