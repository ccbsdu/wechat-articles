### 国有资产分配机制对居民收入和消费的影响
---

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant NationalEconomy as 国民经济
    participant StateAssets as 国有资产
    participant StateOwnedEnterprises as 国有企业
    participant PublicFinance as 公共财政
    participant Residents as 居民

    NationalEconomy ->> StateAssets: 国有经济占据重要地位
    note over NationalEconomy,StateAssets: 国有资产在国民经济中占比高
    activate StateAssets
    StateAssets -->> StateAssets: 国有资产收益分配机制待完善
    note left of StateAssets: 收益分配机制不完善
    StateAssets -->> StateOwnedEnterprises: 收益用于再投资
    note right of StateOwnedEnterprises: 国有企业再投资
    StateAssets -->> PublicFinance: 收益补充公共财政
    note right of PublicFinance: 补充公共财政
    StateAssets -->> Residents: 直接分配给居民比例偏低
    note over Residents: 直接分配比例较低

    StateAssets ->> StateAssets: 缺乏“全民所有制”实现形式
    note left of StateAssets: 缺乏有效分配机制
    deactivate StateAssets
    StateOwnedEnterprises ->> StateOwnedEnterprises: 盈利能力和经营效率待提升
    note left of StateOwnedEnterprises: 部分企业效率不高
    StateOwnedEnterprises -->> StateAssets: 间接影响国有资产收益水平
    
    StateAssets -->> Residents: 居民难充分分享增值红利
     note over StateAssets,Residents: 居民难享增值红利

    Residents -->> Residents: 可支配收入减少
    note left of Residents: 影响居民收入
    Residents -->> Residents: 所有者认同感和获得感降低
       note left of Residents: 削弱所有者认同感和获得感
    Residents -->> Residents: 降低参与经济发展/扩大消费积极性
       note left of Residents: 降低经济发展/扩大消费积极性

    Residents -->> Residents: 缺乏有效分享机制
    note over Residents: 缺乏分享机制

```
