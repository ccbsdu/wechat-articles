### 营造良好消费环境
---

```mermaid

sequenceDiagram
    participant Government as 政府
    participant ConsumerProtection as 消费者保护
    participant NewConsumption as 新型消费
    participant RealEstateMarket as 房地产市场
    participant BusinessEnvironment as 营商环境
    participant Residents as 居民

    rect rgba(173, 216, 230, 0.3) # 改善消费环境，浅蓝色背景
        Government ->> ConsumerProtection: 改善消费环境
        note over Government,ConsumerProtection: 提振消费信心
        activate ConsumerProtection
        ConsumerProtection -->> ConsumerProtection: 加强消费者权益保护
        note left of ConsumerProtection: 完善消费维权机制
        ConsumerProtection -->> ConsumerProtection: 打击假冒伪劣商品
        note left of ConsumerProtection: 规范市场秩序
        ConsumerProtection -->> Residents: 营造安全、放心、诚信的消费环境
        note left of Residents: 提振居民消费信心
        deactivate ConsumerProtection
    end

    rect rgba(144, 238, 144, 0.3) # 培育新型消费，浅绿色背景
        Government ->> NewConsumption: 培育壮大新型消费
        note over Government,NewConsumption: 拓展消费空间
        activate NewConsumption
        NewConsumption -->> NewConsumption: 支持发展数字消费、绿色消费
        note left of NewConsumption: 支持健康消费、体验消费
        NewConsumption -->> NewConsumption: 创新消费场景
        note left of NewConsumption: 拓展消费空间
        NewConsumption -->> Residents: 满足多样化、个性化、品质化消费需求
        note left of Residents: 激发消费活力
        deactivate NewConsumption
    end

    rect rgba(255, 182, 193, 0.3) # 稳定房地产市场，浅粉色背景
        Government ->> RealEstateMarket: 坚持“房住不炒”定位
        note over Government,RealEstateMarket: 促进房地产市场平稳健康发展
        activate RealEstateMarket
        RealEstateMarket -->> RealEstateMarket: 稳定房地产市场预期
        note left of RealEstateMarket: 防范化解房地产市场风险
        RealEstateMarket -->> Residents: 避免房地产市场过度波动
        note left of Residents: 减少对居民消费的负面影响
        deactivate RealEstateMarket
    end

    rect rgba(255, 255, 153, 0.3) # 优化营商环境，浅黄色背景
        Government ->> BusinessEnvironment: 持续优化营商环境
        note over Government,BusinessEnvironment: 激发市场主体活力
        activate BusinessEnvironment
        BusinessEnvironment -->> BusinessEnvironment: 深入推进“放管服”改革
        note left of BusinessEnvironment: 打破行政垄断和市场壁垒
        BusinessEnvironment -->> BusinessEnvironment: 优化营商环境
        note left of BusinessEnvironment: 激发各类市场主体活力
        BusinessEnvironment -->> Residents: 提振企业投资信心
        note left of Residents: 为经济发展和需求扩张<br>创造良好制度环境
        deactivate BusinessEnvironment
    end
```
