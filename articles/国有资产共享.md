### 提高国有资产收益全民共享程度
---

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant Government as 政府
    participant StateAssets as 国有资产
    participant Residents as 居民
    participant SOEs as 国有企业

    rect rgba(173, 216, 230, 0.3) # 提高利润上缴比例，浅蓝色背景
        Government ->> StateAssets: 提高国有企业利润上缴比例
        note over Government,StateAssets: 显著提高利润上缴比例
        activate StateAssets
        StateAssets -->> StateAssets: 增加国有资本经营预算收入
        note left of StateAssets: 专项用于民生领域
        StateAssets -->> Residents: 增加居民福利
        note right of Residents: 提升社会保障水平
        deactivate StateAssets
    end

    rect rgba(144, 238, 144, 0.3) # 探索收益分配新机制，浅绿色背景
        Government ->> StateAssets: 探索国有资产收益分配新机制
        note over Government,StateAssets: 增加居民财产性收入
        activate StateAssets
        StateAssets -->> StateAssets: 划拨部分国有股权充实社保基金
        note left of StateAssets: 探索分红等多种形式
        StateAssets -->> Residents: 公平分享国有资产增值红利
        note right of Residents: 增强获得感和认同感
        deactivate StateAssets
    end

    rect rgba(255, 182, 193, 0.3) # 深化国企改革，浅粉色背景
        Government ->> SOEs: 深化国有企业改革
        note over Government,SOEs: 提升经营效率和盈利能力
        activate SOEs
        SOEs -->> SOEs: 完善现代企业制度
        note left of SOEs: 优化国有资本布局
        SOEs -->> SOEs: 聚焦主责主业
        note left of SOEs: 提升经营效率和盈利能力
        SOEs -->> StateAssets: 提供更坚实的收益分配基础
        note right of StateAssets: 支持国有资产收益分配
        deactivate SOEs
    end
```
