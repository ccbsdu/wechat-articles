### 增强居民有效需求的必要性和重要性
---

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant Residents as 居民/民生
    participant Consumption as 消费
    participant EconomicGrowth as 经济增长
    participant SupplySide as 供给侧
    participant RiskManagement as 风险管理

    rect rgba(173, 216, 230, 0.3) # 稳增长阶段，浅蓝色背景
        Residents ->> Consumption: 增强居民有效需求
        note over Residents,Consumption: 增强居民消费需求
        activate Consumption
        Consumption -->> EconomicGrowth: 稳增长的关键支撑
        note right of EconomicGrowth: 消费是经济增长的“压舱石”
        EconomicGrowth -->> EconomicGrowth: 扩大内需，稳定经济增长
        note left of EconomicGrowth: 应对外部冲击，实现高质量发展
        EconomicGrowth -->> EconomicGrowth: 对冲外部需求萎缩
        note left of EconomicGrowth: 确保经济运行在合理区间
        deactivate Consumption
    end

    rect rgba(144, 238, 144, 0.3) # 促改革阶段，浅绿色背景
        Residents ->> SupplySide: 增强居民有效需求
        note over Residents,SupplySide: 倒逼供给侧改革
        activate SupplySide
        SupplySide -->> SupplySide: 推动产业结构优化升级
        note left of SupplySide: 满足多样化、高品质消费需求
        SupplySide -->> SupplySide: 加快技术创新、产品升级、服务提升
        note left of SupplySide: 推动创新驱动、消费驱动
        deactivate SupplySide
    end

    rect rgba(255, 182, 193, 0.3) # 惠民生阶段，浅粉色背景
        Residents ->> Residents: 增强居民有效需求
        note over Residents,EconomicGrowth: 惠民生的根本体现
        activate Residents
        Residents -->> Residents: 提高居民收入水平
        note right of Residents: 改善民生保障
        Residents -->> Residents: 提升消费环境
        note right of Residents: 增强获得感、幸福感、安全感
        deactivate Residents
    end

    rect rgba(255, 255, 153, 0.3) # 防风险阶段，浅黄色背景
        Residents ->> RiskManagement: 增强居民有效需求
        note over Residents,RiskManagement: 防风险的重要屏障
        activate RiskManagement
        RiskManagement -->> RiskManagement: 构建强大的国内市场
        note left of RiskManagement: 降低对外部市场的依赖
        RiskManagement -->> RiskManagement: 提升经济发展的自主性和安全性
        note left of RiskManagement: 增强抵御外部风险的能力
        deactivate RiskManagement
    end


```
